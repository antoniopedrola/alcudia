Es Coll Baix Tour

From €390

2.5 - 4 Hours

All inclusive: Es Coll Baix. 2.5h option is only one stop at Es Coll Baix (1h45). 4h option includes a second stop and entering the cave by boat.

Discover one of Mallorca's most iconic hidden gems. Es Coll Baix is a pristine, virgin beach accessible primarily by boat, famous for its grey pebbles and shimmering turquoise waters.

  • Professional Skipper included
